Indica

Indica cartridges are built for winding down. Deep body relaxation, a calm headspace, and a smooth draw that eases you into the evening. If you're done for the day and want something that helps you decompress without overthinking it, Indica is your cart.

Hybrid

Hybrid cartridges cover most occasions. Balanced effects that lift your mood without putting you on the couch — and grounded enough to keep you functional. Whether you're socializing, creating, or just filling time, a good Hybrid cart handles it.

Sativa

Sativa cartridges are built for energy and focus. Uplifting, clear-headed, and best for daytime use when you want to stay productive or creative. These carts keep you moving without the heaviness of an Indica.

Live Resin cartridges are extracted from fresh-frozen plant material to preserve the full terpene profile. Richer flavour, more complex aroma, and a more layered effect than standard distillate. If you want the best-tasting cart in the lineup, this is it.
